Our Services

We work with start-ups, tech or non-tech companies to help them to solve technical problems in different areas of the project. We help them upgrade or create new products and digital services, taking the technical hassle off our clients' shoulders and help them build a scalable, cost-effective and time-consuming solution.

Web Development

We build web applications ranging from lightweight landing pages or MVPs to complex Ecommerce sites or FinTech solutions. Our team specializes in Python, Flask, AngularJS. Most demanding web applications can be satisfied with our tech stack.

Mobile App Development

We build mobile applcations with cross-platform development. Our team specializes in Cordova and Ionic. Progressive apps, offer faster and cost effective development for both Android and iOS.

UX/UI Design

We design to transform your ideas into fantastic interfaces, where users can be visual stimulated and can interact with the application as easy and intuitive a world inside of an app can allows us to develop. We create research-based, scalable interfaces following the best UI/UX practices.

Hardware Development

We design and build hardware. We turn the needs of interaction with the real world in a digital way. We fast prototype your MVP or projects for testing and concept-proof as well as build you a market ready product. Our team specilizes with controllers such as Raspberry Pis or microcontrollers.

IoT Systems

We design and build Internet of Things devices. We build your hardware with sensors connected to internet and we build the web/mobile application to manage, monitor and control your hardware and sensors. Our team specializes with most up-to-date technologies such as Amazon Web Services - AWS IoT

Data Scraping

We build scripts to collect unorganized data all over internet to organize it and store it in databases. Clients find it usefull to gather information to develop new products, for market studies, among others. Our team specializes with Python, Pandas, any database, excel, csv among others.
