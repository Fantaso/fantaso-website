About Fantaso

Fantaso Digital Services was founded in 2018 for the emerging tech scene in Berlin. Fantaso started as an audio/visual studio back in 2008 in Venezuela, however it pivoted due to the largely emerging tech scene that has been growing in Berlin for the past years. We saw the need in many startups and tech companies that wanted to prototype or develop products fast and effective without the need of worrying about the technical hassles at a fair price.

We are a team of dreamers, visionaries and makers specialized in engineering, informatics and designing that always break the paradigms to bring fantasy to life.

We help startups, tech companies, freelancers, engineers and designers to develop digital solutions from start until completion. We manage your project, develop it, test it. We hand you a turnkey solution ready for you to start using it. Our projects can range from software and hardware development to UI/UX design.

  • We develop from web landing page or Ecommerce to more complex web/mobile applications such instagram or xxxxx.
  • We can fast prototype hardware with raspberry or make a market-ready product as well as integrating your hardware with a web/mobile app as an IoT system.
  • We can also design the graphical user interface and experience of your application.

We are huge believers in working projects where the innovations we create and make, they help human kind to be one step close to less dependency on intrisic needs such as: 1.water, 2.energy, 3.food, 4.shelter, 5.information.

If any of this is of your interest or It could benefit your business please get in touch with over email or phone.
